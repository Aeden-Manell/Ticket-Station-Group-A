About Ticket Station

Ticket Station is a dynamic and innovative online platform that empowers event enthusiasts to seamlessly book tickets for a wide range of events, from concerts and festivals to sporting events and cultural exhibitions.

Our Story

Ticket Station was founded by four ambitious and determined black South African students: Aeden, Keneilwe, Velaphi & Thabang. Hailing from underprivileged backgrounds, they were driven by a shared passion for events and a desire to make a difference in their community.

With limited resources and unwavering determination, they pooled their knowledge and skills to create a platform that would address the challenges they faced as event-goers. They recognized the need for a user-friendly, affordable, and accessible ticketing system that would connect event organizers with potential attendees.

We're a dynamic team of creatives people innovation

Impact

Since its inception, Ticket Station has become a trusted ticketing provider for countless events across South Africa. We have empowered thousands of event organizers to reach a wider audience and increase their revenue.

Ticket Station is more than just a ticketing platform. It is a symbol of hope, innovation, and the power of dreams. We invite you to join us on our journey to make live events accessible and enjoyable for all.

Values

  • Inclusion: We believe that everyone should have access to the joy and excitement of live events.
  • Innovation: We embrace technology to create a cutting-edge ticketing solution that meets the evolving needs of our users.
  • Integrity: We operate with transparency and honesty, building trust with our customers and partners.
  • Community: We are deeply rooted in our community and strive to make a positive contribution.

    • Mission

    Ticket Station is committed to:

    1. Providing a seamless and convenient ticketing experience for event enthusiasts.

    2. Creating opportunities for young people from underprivileged backgrounds to pursue their passions and make a positive impact.

    3. Empowering event organizers with a powerful platform to promote and sell their events.
