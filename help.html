Payment Information

At Ticket Station, we strive to provide a seamless and convenient payment experience for our customers. We accept major credit and debit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.

Refunds and Reschedules

In the event of a cancelled show, how will I receive my refunded?

If you paid via Credit Card, your funds will automatically be returned to the card that you purchased with. If you paid via EFT or Ozow, you will be prompted to enter your banking details.

If you paid via Pick n Pay, you will receive a new reference number to take instore to receive your cash refund at any Pick n Pay store nationwide. • No races will be refunded.

How long does it take to receive a refund?

Once you have processed your refund using the self-refund option for a cancelled or rescheduled event or race, your refund will usually take 7-10 working days to reflect.

Please check the purchase date on your statement to see if the refunded funds reflect on that date.
